❓ COMMON QUESTIONS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Will this detect the honeypot?
A: Unlikely. Every response is generated by AI in real-time, not pre-scripted.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: ~$0.0001 per command. 1000 commands = $0.10. Varies by command length.

Q: Can I use a free LLM?
A: Yes! Groq Llama-3 is free and 10x faster. Just change the model in code.

Q: What happens if I delete a file?
A: Currently, it "deletes" but state doesn't persist yet. Phase 2 fixes this.

Q: Can I run this on production?
A: NO! Only in isolated environments. This is a honeypot, not a real system.

Q: How do I stop it?
A: Press Ctrl+C in the main.py terminal.

Q: How do I see what attackers did?
A: Check logs/ghostnet.log or view the dashboard.

Q: Can multiple attackers connect?
A: Yes! Multi-threaded, supports unlimited concurrent connections.


💡 TIPS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
